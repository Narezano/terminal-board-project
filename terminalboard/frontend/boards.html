ABOUT TERMINALBOARD Retro web chatroom vibes. Clean. Simple. Fast.

TerminalBoard is a retro-themed social space inspired by classic terminal UIs and early internet communities — built around real-time chatrooms, lightweight navigation, and a clean “no clutter” experience.

What it is
  • Multi-room chat experience with a terminal-inspired aesthetic.
  • Accounts + authentication for access control.
  • Designed to feel like a “living” retro space, not a modern feed.
What it is not
  • No boards. No threads. No posts.
  • No “infinite scroll” content feed.
  • No extra features here that could affect chat/admin.
Heads up

There are currently no CENSORSHIP/PROFANITY FILTER feature yet. Will be updated in the coming months.
